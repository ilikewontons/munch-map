About Munch Map

Munch Map is a student-driven initiative created by FLAME students to provide a comprehensive guide to the best cafes around the FLAME University campus. Whether you're looking for a quick coffee break, a healthy snack, or a place to hang out with friends, Munch Map is your go-to resource for finding the perfect café experience on campus.

Each café's profile is crafted based on essential questions that students want to know, such as the favorite café on campus, the healthiest food options, and the price range from least expensive to most expensive. We've also included recommendations on the most popular food and beverage items from each café, helping you make informed choices.
